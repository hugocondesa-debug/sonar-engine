# F2 · Momentum e breadth

> Sub-índice do Ciclo Financeiro — capítulo 8 do manual (Manual_Ciclo_Financeiro_COMPLETO).

### 8.1 Porque momentum matter
Momentum — a tendência de prices que moveram numa direção continuarem nessa direção no curto-médio prazo — é um dos facts mais robust em finance. Empiricamente documented across centuries e countries.

Para classificação do ciclo, momentum é coincident indicator crítico. Strong momentum = robust market regime. Breaking momentum = transition signal. Combined with breadth, momentum paints detailed picture de market health.

> *Shiller-style excess volatility argues that prices deviate from fundamentals. Momentum is the mechanism — prices continue deviating before reverting. In this sense, momentum measures persistence of deviation.*

### 8.2 Price momentum — basics
**Simple moving average relationships**

- Price \> 200-day MA: long-term uptrend

- Price \> 50-day MA: short-term uptrend

- 50-day MA \> 200-day MA: "golden cross"

- 50-day MA \< 200-day MA: "death cross"

- Simple but surprisingly effective

**Trend strength indicators**

- ADX (Average Directional Index)

- Values \> 25: strong trend

- Values \< 20: weak/no trend

- Momentum quality metric

**Rate of change (ROC)**

- Percent price change over N periods

- 3-month ROC: short-term momentum

- 12-month ROC: long-term momentum

- Widely used in academic momentum literature

**Classical momentum studies**

- Jegadeesh-Titman (1993): 3-12 month winners continue

- Fama-French (2012): momentum factor robust globally

- Asness-Frazzini-Pedersen: value + momentum complement

### 8.3 Breadth — how broad-based
**Advance-Decline line**

- Cumulative daily (advancers - decliners)

- Should rise with market in healthy uptrends

- Divergence warning: index rising but AD falling

- Most common breadth indicator

**New highs - New lows**

- Stocks making new 52-week highs vs lows

- NH \> NL sustained: healthy uptrend

- NH declining even as index rising: narrowing breadth = warning

- Late-stage bull markets typically narrow

**Percent stocks above key moving averages**

- Percent S&P 500 above 200-day MA

- Percent above 50-day MA

- Percent above 20-day MA

- 80%+ above 200-day MA: strong uptrend

- \< 30%: bearish territory

**McClellan Oscillator**

- EMA differences of advances-declines

- Classic overbought/oversold indicator

- Extremes: signal reversals potential

**Sector breadth**

- How many sectors in uptrend

- Broader participation = more sustainable

- Leadership quality important

### 8.4 Breadth divergences — leading signals
**Classic divergence**

Index makes new high but breadth indicator fails to confirm. Historical pattern for market tops.

- Late-2021: index peaks but breadth narrowing

- 2000 tech peak: narrow leadership

- 2007 pre-crisis: breadth deteriorating

- Warning signal, though timing imperfect

**Reverse divergence**

Index makes new low but breadth improving. Often signals bottom approaching.

- Mar 2009: breadth started improving before index bottom

- Oct 2022: similar pattern

- Breadth leading recovery signal

**Capitulation indicators**

- Percent stocks below 52-week low extremes

- Oversold readings via McClellan

- Typically precede bottoms

### 8.5 Cross-asset momentum
**Risk-on momentum**

- Equity strength

- Credit spreads tightening

- Commodities strength

- USD weakness

- Combined risk-on regime

**Risk-off momentum**

- Equity weakness

- Credit spreads widening

- USD strength

- Bonds rally

- Combined risk-off regime

**Cross-asset momentum synthesis**

- SONAR tracks 8 major asset aggregates

- Equity, bonds (duration), bonds (credit), USD, commodities, crypto, VIX

- Momentum signal for each

- Aggregation to overall risk regime

### 8.6 Crypto momentum — high-velocity
**Crypto-specific momentum features**

- 24/7 trading amplifies momentum

- Leverage via perpetuals accelerates

- Social media amplifiers

- Faster cycles than traditional

**Key crypto momentum indicators**

- **200-day MA for BTC/ETH:** long-term trend separator

- **Bitcoin Mayer Multiple:** Price / 200-day MA. \> 2.4 = bubble, \< 0.6 = bottom

- **Crypto fear & greed index:** composite sentiment

- **Funding rates momentum:** sustained positive = bulls paying premium

**On-chain momentum**

- Active addresses trend

- Exchange inflow/outflow

- Long-term holder supply percentage

- Miner selling pressure

### 8.8 Technical breakdowns
Certain technical patterns historically precede regime changes.

**Key levels violations**

- 200-day MA breached

- Prior cycle lows breached

- Trend line breakdowns

**Volume patterns**

- Distribution days (high volume declines)

- Climax volumes at tops

- Selling climax at bottoms

**Volatility regime shifts**

- VIX jump from compressed level

- Persistent high vol regime

- Transition signal

### 8.9 F2 Momentum composite
**Structure**

Price momentum (40%) + cross-asset (25%) + crypto (15%) + breadth (15%) + RE (5%).

**Interpretation**

- F2 \> 75: broadly positive momentum (Euphoria territory, or late Optimism)

- F2 55-75: positive momentum

- F2 30-55: mixed momentum (Caution territory)

- F2 \< 30: broadly negative momentum (Stress)

**Signal quality**

- When momentum AND breadth align: strong signal

- When momentum but breadth narrows: warning

- When price reversal but breadth improving: potential inflection
